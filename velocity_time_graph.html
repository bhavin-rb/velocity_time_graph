Acceleration is change in velocity over time. In other words it is the slope of the the triangle formed between time 0s and 10s, having vertices (height) of 20.

From the plotted graph, v0 = 0 m/s, v1 = 20 m/s, t0 = 0s and t1 = 10s. Hence acceleration is given by:

In [141]:
# lets define our variables
v0 = 0
t0 = 0
v1 = 20
t1 = 10

# for printing superscript for S.I Unit of acceleration
SUP = str.maketrans("0123456789", "")

# acceleration, a formula (equation)
a = (v1 - v0)/(t1 - t0)

f"Acceleration of the car is {a}" +" "f"m/s2".translate(SUP)
Out[141]:
'Acceleration of the car is 2.0 m/s'

Deceleration is negative acceleration, when the car is coming to a stop. From the plot (graph), the car is slowing down from 20s and comes to a stop on 40s. That means we have negative slop formed between triangle 20s and 40s havinf vertices (height) of 20.

We use the same formula of acceleration.

From the plotted graph, v0 = 20 m/s, v1 = 0 m/s, t0 = 20s and t1 = 40s. The initial velocity, v0 is 20m/s and the final velocity, v1 is 0m/s as the car comes to a stop. This is between period of time,20s and 40s.

Total Ditsnace travelled by a Car is equal to the area under the graph. The plot (graph) is a Trapezium and area of Trapezium is given by:

b = 40 - 0   # from 0s to 40s
h = 20

area = ((a + b) * h)/2

f"Total distance travelled by the car is {area} m"
